Sports

Gaelic Football:

Gaelic football, commonly referred to as football or Gaelic, is an Irish team sport. It is played between two teams of 15 players on a rectangular grass pitch. The objective of the sport is to score by kicking or punching the ball into the other team's goals, which counts as 3 points, or between two upright posts above the goals and over a crossbar 2.5 metres above the ground, which counts as 1 point.

I have great interest in this sport as I have played it since I was 11 years old. It is a great way to keep fit and to make new friends and I would highly recommend it to any person. The sport is also played in other countires where there may be lots of Irish living.
